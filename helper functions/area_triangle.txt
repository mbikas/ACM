   ### Area of a triangle is A(x1,y1) B(x2,y2) & C(x3,y3) are given ###

   Area of ABC = 1/2 * (  x1*(y2-y3) + x2*(y3-y1) + x3*(y1-y2)  )
               
               = 1/2 * ( (x1*y2+x2*y3+x3*y1) - (y1*x2+y2*x3+y3*x1)  )
